Clay Specialist: 
This individual possesses a kinjutsu that allows them to freely manipulate clay into explosives.  These creations are kneaded through various means around the body, and range in their construct by the amount of chakra used to create them and the skill of the user.

 


Dual Manipulation!: With multiple manipulative "mouths" on their bodies, these individuals are able to simultaneously craft two explosives at once.
