Deft Handseals: 
This person is trained to be able to easily contort their hands into handsigns.
Note: Max seven handsigns in a round



Swift Handseals: 
With swift accuracy this person is able to easily form handsigns with quick ease.
Note: Max twelve handsigns in a round



Amazing Handseals: 
With an amazing degree of skill, this person is able to effortlessly form a quick series of handsigns
Note: Max twenty-two handsigns in a round

Blinding Handseals: 
Simply a blur to the naked eye, this person is able to effortlessly form handsigns with amazing accuracy.
Note: Fourty five handsigns in a round
