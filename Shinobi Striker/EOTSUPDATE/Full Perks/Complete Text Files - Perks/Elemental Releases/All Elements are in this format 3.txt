Elemental Training Level 1: 
This user is a rookie in their elemental training.  They are capable of harnessing it's nature, but suffer significant chakra loss.

Elemental Training Level 2: 
This individual is highly trained in utilizing their respective nature.  They are capable of unleashing this element at full power, without significant chakra loss!



Elemental Training Level 3: 
This individual is a master of their elemental affinity.  They are capable of unleashing every aspect of their nature, and with it comes the dominancy of years of training.  Their power is significantly increased, all extra chakra conversion null and void.
